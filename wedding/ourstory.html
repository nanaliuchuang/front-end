    OUR STORY

    The Bride

    Nguyen Bich Ngoc, a Vietnamese fashion designer currently living and working in New York City. She was born and raised in Danang City, Vietnam. She has been into drawing and designing since she was a small kid, but the idea of becoming a fashion designer is not always supported in Vietnam since it is new and risky. Therefore Ngoc never opens up about her passion which makes her shy and an introverted person. But her passion motivates her to do well in school with a hope of scholarship overseas where more opportunity present and support her passion. Her hard work has got her scholarship to NYU and it also proves to her parents she can pursue her passion. Her journey to the U.S began.

    The groom

    David Blane, a businessman working in New York City. David was born and raised in Virginia. He is an outgoing, social person, he plays multiple sports in highschool. He has a special power of making friends with everyone. He wanted to attend NYU studying financing, seek opportunity and start networking with people to eventually start his business. He has everything planned out but it hasn’t gone as planned. He started off college strong but he got carried away by partying. His grade goes down non stop the second semester and has lost his scholarship.

    How to meet

    Now after knowing their background you might ask how can these two totally different people meet and fall in love with each other. Well, they got into a class together and got assigned as a group for a semester project. At first Ngoc only shared project related stuff with David, and David as an extrovert he got to talk about everything but the project. David is not doing any work and leaving Ngoc to do everything. One day Ngoc couldn’t handle it anymore and just had a long yelling session at David, he had never seen her talk so much and been so angry before. He couldn’t say anything but sorry, the next day David reached out to Ngoc to ask her about the project and he got her food as a way of saying sorry. They started talking more and more and finally they fell in love with each other.
