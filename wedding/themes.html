    • Theme Traditional Vietnamese wedding theme with a mix of modern western wedding at a beach in Danang.

    Vision
  • The wedding will take place outdoors at a private beach section of the resort. It will be designed in red, white and pink.
  • Red represents the lucky color of Vietnam as it will bring luck and happiness into the marriage.
  • White is the color presenting the pureness of their love and it presents a luxurious feel.
  • White is the color that is traditionally used in western weddings.
  • Pink is the mix of red and white it representing the two cultures that are being blended in this special event.
