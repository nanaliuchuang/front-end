    Reading for Ceremony

  • Groom to the Bride: The first given opportunity I was given is a chance to go to NYU to pursue my career. But I had wasted it on partying and not focusing on studying, I got off-track and thought I would not be able to make it again. But then I was given a second chance when I met Ngoc. I could never forget the day she got angry and yelled at me for not contributing to the project. I was in shock. I never heard her talk so much and so angry before. That day changed my life forever, she has helped me get on track again. She made me the person I am today and I am thankful for that. And today I was given the second opportunity, the biggest opportunity ever is the opportunity to go down the path of the rest of my life with Ngoc. Ngoc will you marry me?

  • Bride to Groom: Before I answer David’s question. I will tell you about my first impression of David. It wasn’t a very good one, I saw him as a boy that went to college just to party and gets by, I have no memory of the day I got angry with him, cause I was too angry. But after that, he tried to interact more and more with me to make up for the missing part of his assignment. I later found out that he is a pretty charming boy, we kept in touch after the assignment and we started hanging out, he helped me a lot too, I was shy and don’t have too many friends, but he has always been with me, support me all the way, help me with social interaction and seeking opportunity. I wouldn’t be myself today without him either. My answer to your answer is yes, please take me all the way until the last day of my life with you.
